Myopia Management — Sunnyvale Location Note
Our Sunnyvale location currently offers Atropine, MiSight, and Stellest myopia management programs. Ortho-K (corneal reshaping) is not available at Sunnyvale at this time. Axial length measurement equipment is not yet available — patients may visit our Santa Clara location for axial length measurements a few times per year. Axial length capability is anticipated at Sunnyvale by 2027.
